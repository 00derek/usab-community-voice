# USAB Community Feedback Meeting — April 18, 2026

**Attendees**: Derek Chang, Justin Rogers, John Ruger, Michelle Cruz, Cora

**Purpose**: Align on key community issues, prioritize improvements, and define actionable next steps

---

## Overview

This meeting was called to review a community-submitted issues document, clarify open questions, and establish concrete action items with accountability. The focus was on identifying actionable next steps — not finalizing solutions on the call.

Issues were grouped into three themes: **transparency**, **player welfare**, and **tournament operations**.

---

## What We Discussed

### 1. Communication — *High Priority*

The community raised concerns about response times to inquiries. Justin Rogers described his daily triage of the contact inbox and will assign complex questions to the appropriate staff.

**SLA Established**:
- Acknowledge within **24 hours**
- Full response within **48 hours**

To reduce repetitive inquiries, USAB will develop:
- FAQ pages (tournaments, selection, etc.)
- Improved website content
- Newsletter and broader communication strategy (under consideration)

---

### 2. Rankings System — *In Progress*

Current rankings are produced manually, but monthly updates are now running consistently. USAB is in active vendor discussions to move toward full automation.

Key details:
- Target delivery: **~4–6 months** (subject to refinement)
- Requirements: duplicate account handling, play-up rule support
- Community members offered technical resources and existing tooling as a reference

**Agreed**: Use formal vendor bidding — not volunteer-led builds — to avoid perceived conflicts and ensure continuity.

---

### 3. Event Scheduling

The community requested earlier publication of next-season schedules to aid travel planning.

**Goal**: Publish major events **~1 year in advance**

Current constraints include venue sourcing and coordination. An internal follow-up with an update is expected within **30 days**.

---

### 4. Event Transparency

The community wants visibility into how host clubs and venues are selected.

**Agreed**: USAB will improve transparency around:
- Which clubs submitted bids
- How selection decisions are made
- Published selection criteria

---

### 5. Junior Nationals Hosting — *Under Exploration*

The current model (convention centers) is costly and labor-intensive. Large club facilities were proposed as an alternative but may also carry significant costs. Both models will be evaluated for feasibility.

Justin Rogers will analyze the financial implications with Mary and report findings.

---

### 6. Junior Development Direction — *Strategic Topic*

The core goal was reaffirmed: **develop athletes for international success** (Olympic and Pan Am levels).

The current system needs reassessment due to:
- Changing athlete development pathways
- Younger internationally competing players entering the field

A broader stakeholder discussion — including parents, volunteers, and athletes — is planned to revisit the junior structure and selection format.

---

### 7. Selection Event Rules & Format

The following items will be reviewed by the **High Performance Committee (HPC)**:
- Retirement rule
- Event format
- Seeding approach (including world junior and adult rankings)

The group recognized that the current system may disadvantage internationally active players.

Justin Rogers will bring Derek's document and community feedback to the HPC within **1–2 weeks**.

---

### 8. Website Improvements

New staff support has been added. Focus areas include:
- Historical records (teams, results)
- Clearer public resources and documentation
